## Methodological Framework

The standard approach involves three phases:

1. **Field survey** - Surface collection and remote sensing
2. **Excavation** - Stratigraphic digging with context recording
3. **Laboratory analysis** - Dating, material composition, comparative study

> "The ground does not lie, but it does not speak plainly either. Interpretation requires both rigor and humility." - Prof. Y. Dever
